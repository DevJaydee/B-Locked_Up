AS3 Vector2 Projects

We were assigned to make multiple little projects with a custom Vector2 file,
the first few projects were to get us to better know Actionscript and Animate.

The final assignment was to make a game with the custom Vector2 Class.
My idea was to make a game similair to Touhou (Bullet Hell, Shoot-Em-Up).

I kinda succeeded, but since collsions are so fun to work with, I kinda stopped trying to waste my time,
and work on other important projects.

Anyway, here's a little synopsis for my game. (keep in mind, this game is in early-alpha)

In a alternate dimension. Where everything excist out of shapes, you play as a Triangle, trying to avoid falling squares,
And make it through to the end.
Your goal is to save other Triangles, and many other shapes
Since the Squares are the evil once who are trying to take over the 2D World.

I made everything into an .EXE File.
